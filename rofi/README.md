# 🌌 Rofi Theme – Presentation Submenu

Welcome to the presentation submenu of my custom **Rofi theme**.
This project is something I’m genuinely proud of — it’s not just a theme, it’s a small piece of identity built into my workflow.

---

## ✨ Overview

This submenu is designed as a **clean and aesthetic presentation layer** inside Rofi.
It allows you to display information, sections, or interactive entries in a way that feels smooth, minimal, and visually coherent with your system.

The goal here wasn’t just functionality — it was to create something that *feels right* when you use it.

---

## 🎨 Dynamic Colors (Pywal Integration)

This theme is fully compatible with **pywal**, meaning:

* Colors automatically adapt to your wallpaper
* The UI stays consistent with your system palette
* Every session can feel slightly different, yet always cohesive

This makes the theme feel alive and deeply integrated into your environment.

---

## 🚀 Future Plans (Matugen Support)

I’m currently planning to extend compatibility to **matugen**.

The idea is to:

* Push dynamic theming even further
* Improve consistency across more applications
* Experiment with more advanced color pipelines

This is coming soon — I want this theme to evolve and stay modern!
